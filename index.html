FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

It is recommended to visit the dentist every 6 months for a routine check-up and cleaning. Regular visits help detect dental issues early and keep your smile healthy.

Modern root canal treatments are safe and virtually painless. At Dental Planet Elite, we use advanced techniques to ensure maximum comfort.

Children should visit the dentist by their first birthday or when their first tooth appears. Early visits help prevent cavities and promote lifelong oral health.

With proper care, dental implants can last 15–20 years or even a lifetime. Regular check-ups and good oral hygiene are essential for long-term success.

Both braces and aligners work effectively. Aligners are more comfortable and invisible, while braces are ideal for complex cases. Our orthodontist will recommend the best option for you.
